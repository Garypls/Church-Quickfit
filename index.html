How to Import a Fitting

Follow these steps to import a fitting into EVE Online:

  1. Click the "Copy Fit" button next to the desired fitting.
  2. Open EVE Online and go to the fitting window.
  3. Click the "Import & Export" arrow (located at the bottom left of the fitting window next to "browse").
  4. Select "Import from Clipboard" to add the fitting to your saved fittings.
